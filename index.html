Tabassum Kakar

Tabassum Kakar is with the Data Science Program at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. Her research focuses on designing interactive visual analytics and human-computer interaction (HCI). She has experience in conducting qualitative and quantitative research to design and validate visualizations and analytics for the exploration and investigation of semi-structured data.

Tabassum is completing her PhD, and holds a Master of Science degree in Data Science from Worcester Polytechnic Institute. She earned her Bachelor of Science degree from GIK Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology, Pakistan in 2011.
