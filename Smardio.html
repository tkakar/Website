Design Challenge - Overview and Goal

While annotations such as adding notes, bookmarks, and highlights are useful in reading books and many ebooks app provide these features. Not many audiobook players, with the exception of a few, allow users to annotate a book as they are listening. One such app Libby provides the features of annotations.

The goal of this design is to improve Libby by making it highly interactive and personal audiobook mobile app that allows users to bookmark, highlight excerpts, and add annotations.


Opportunities for Design Improvement

I experienced Libby as a user and identified the following pros and cons related to adding annoations to a book.

Key Pluses of Libby

  • Track breakdown by time segment.
  • Visual feedback of annotations.
  • Activity history with a summary of annotations.
  • Color options for highlight.

Design Opportunities

  • Bookmark does not allow adding remarks.
  • Unable to rename a bookmark.
  • Highlighting is not intuitive (difficult to find how to highlight).
  • Highlight is activated only when track is playing.
  • Highlight is only added to the audio at the current player location and cannot not be added to an excerpt (audio segment).

Research of Other Audio and Ebook Apps

To have a better understanding of the required features, I explored a few apps.

  • Amazon Kindle and Apple Books app to see the interactive features of reading an eBook and how bookmarks, highlights, and notes are added in each of these apps.
  • Audible, Apple's Podcast and Books Apps, Pandora, and Soundcloud to take a closer look at audio players and their capabilities, features, and shortcomings.


Features Brainstorming and User Flow

Features identified from researching different aduio and ebook apps.

Identified Features for a Good User Experience

Based on my research and brainstorming, below is the list of features that I prioritized to be included in our audiobook player app.

  • Highlight an audio by selecting excerpts.
  • View text related to the highlight.
  • Share highlighted text/audio with others.
  • Add Bookmarks.
  • Add Notes (both using text and by recording audio notes).
  • View and Edit the Annotation (Activity) History.
  • Search in the audiobook for a word.
  • View summary of activity for each chapter in a book.
  • Dashboard that allows user to set goals and monitor their reading routine.
  • View summary of annotation activity for each book (currently reading) in the dashboard.

Additional Features and Food for Thought

  • Adding dictionary access in the app.
  • Audio to text conversion for audio notes.
  • Zoom in/out on the track player (to control track breakdown).
  • Attention check points set by the user to help them stay focused while listening to a chapter.
  • Combine audiobook and eBook in th app by playing the audio as well as displaying the text (controlled by users, whichever they prefer).
